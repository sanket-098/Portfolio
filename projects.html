Vehicle Accident Alert System

Mentor: Prof. Vaishali Katkar | Team Size: 4

Designed and developed an intelligent accident detection and alert system using Arduino Nano, ADXL345 accelerometer, GPS (NEO6M), and GSM (SIM800L) modules to automatically detect collisions and send real-time location alerts to emergency contacts. Integrated Wi-Fi (ESP8266) for contact management and manual emergency/cancel buttons to prevent false alarms. The system ensures faster emergency response by providing instant accident notifications with GPS coordinates, enhancing passenger safety in vehicles. Focused on cost-effective design, real-time communication, and reliability, with successful testing of impact sensitivity, location accuracy, and transmission speed.

Intelligent Vehicle Detection & Alert System for Hilly Areas

Mentor: Dr. Kalpana Pawase | Team Size: 4

This invention is related to the field of automated road safety systems, particularly in hilly and accident-prone terrains, using sensor-based detection and signaling mechanisms. More specifically, it is a system to detect approaching vehicles and alert other road users via visual and audio signals to prevent accidents.

Smart Bluetooth-Controlled Door Lock System

Mentor: Dr. Aswathy M A | Team Size: 4

Developed a secure Bluetooth-based smart door lock using Arduino Uno, HC-05, and a solenoid lock. Implemented wireless lock/unlock control via mobile app with proper driver circuitry, diode protection, and dedicated solenoid power. Strengthened skills in embedded programming, Bluetooth communication, and hardware software integration.
